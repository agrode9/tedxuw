In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event.

TEDxUWMadison is a student run group at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, and is for students only. The past few years, we have sold out our tickets within minutes and being a member ensures attendance to the events. In addition to hosting our big event, our organization meets multiple times per month to watch TED talks, go bowling, eat pizza and other fun events!
